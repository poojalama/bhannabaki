Grew up in Nepal, now I find myself pursuing Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at Old Dominion University in Norfolk, Virginia. Web development and data science are some of my interests at the moment. I have used programming languages such as Javascript, C++, Java and now I'm currently teaching myself how to code in python. I started realizing working directly with different types of people from different fields teaches you so much and shows a lot of perspectives when I started participating in hackathons. My favourite hackathon has to be the very last one I participated in and that was Techtogether Boston 2020 where my team and I were able to win the "Environment Sustainability Hack". I am looking forward for more opportunities like these where I can work with teams, learn from them while also teaching them what I know and build things that matter.
